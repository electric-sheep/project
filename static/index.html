Massively-Multiplayer Online College Cramming

It has been proven a long time ago that the most natural way humans learn is through play. For children this comes natural but as we grow up, we seem to forget this.

College Battle takes competitive massively-multiplayer online gaming and places it into the context of schools.

Students compete in realtime around the clock for the best score. Each question lasts 10 seconds and the speed of answer counts.
